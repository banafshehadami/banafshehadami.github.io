About

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of South Florida. My research focuses on building secure and trustworthy intelligent systems at the intersection of machine learning, computer vision, and healthcare AI.

I develop methods for biometric presentation attack detection, remote physiological signal modeling, and robust anti-spoofing under real-world operating conditions.
