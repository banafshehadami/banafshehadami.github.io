Banafsheh Adami

Ph.D. Student in Computer Science · University of South Florida · Tampa, FL

GitHub · Google Scholar · ORCID · LinkedIn

My research combines machine learning, computer vision, and healthcare AI to build secure and trustworthy intelligent systems. Current interests include biometric anti-spoofing, remote physiological signal modeling (rPPG and rECG), and presentation attack detection under real-world conditions.

I actively contribute to the research community through peer review for conferences (ICASSP, ICIP) and journals (IEEE TIFS, IEEE TBIOM, IEEE Access, Neurocomputing).
