Banafsheh Adami

Computer Vision | Machine Learning | Biometrics

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of South Florida. My research focuses on machine learning, deep learning, and robust biometric AI systems.

Current interests include contactless fingerprint anti-spoofing, heart-rate estimation via rPPG, and real-world trustworthy AI.
